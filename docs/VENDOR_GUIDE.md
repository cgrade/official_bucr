## 1. The big idea

Bucr is built to solve **no‑shows**. When a guest books your restaurant, they place a small **refundable credit deposit**. That deposit makes the booking real:

- **They show up** → their deposit is returned **plus a 3% bonus**, and you’re billed a small **per‑cover fee for each seated head** (so a table of 4 is 4× the fee).
- **They don’t show up** → they forfeit **40%** of the deposit. **You receive 30% of the original deposit** as **marketing credits** (compensation for the held table), and Bucr keeps 10%.

You never chase deposits, never process refunds, and never lose a fully‑prepped table for nothing. Everything is automatic.

## 2. Getting started

### 2.1 Register your restaurant (4 steps)
From the vendor portal, click **Register your restaurant**:

1. **Owner details** — choose your **country** first (this sets your phone format and currency), then your name, email, phone, and a password.
2. **Business info** — business type, **venue type** (Fine Dining / Upscale Casual / Lounge / Casual — this sets your default deposit), business name, description, cuisines.
3. **Location** — address, city, state, and **pin your exact spot on the map** so diners can find you and get directions. (If you skip the pin, we auto‑geocode your address.)
4. **Documents** — upload your **CAC certificate** and **owner ID** for verification.

### 2.2 Verification (KYC)
After you submit documents, your account is **Pending** until the Bucr team reviews and approves them. Approved venues become visible to diners. You can still set up your menu, hours, and photos while pending.

### 2.3 Sign in
Use your registered email + password. Forgot it? Use **Forgot password** on the sign‑in screen.

## 4. Reservations & check‑in

**Reservations** lists every booking with its status: *Confirmed → Checked‑in → Completed*, or *Cancelled / No‑show*.

### How a booking flows
1. Guest books on the Bucr app/website and places the deposit → you get a **new‑reservation notification** (in‑app + email).
2. The guest arrives.
3. You **check them in** — this is the important step (it triggers the deposit refund + bonus to the guest, and records the cover for billing).

### Checking a guest in — the QR Scanner
Open **QR Scanner**. Three ways to check a guest in:
- **Scan their QR code** (they show it in the Bucr app),
- **Enter their PIN**, or
- **Enter their booking reference**.

On a successful check‑in you’ll see the guest’s name, party size, and visit history. ✅ **Always check guests in** — if you don’t, the guest’s deposit isn’t released and the cover isn’t recorded.

### No‑shows
If a guest never arrives, mark the reservation a **no‑show**. This forfeits 40% of their deposit and credits **30% of the original deposit to your wallet** as marketing credits. (Be fair — only mark genuine no‑shows; it affects guests.)

## 5. Menu management

**Menu** lets you build your menu so diners can see what you offer.

- **Categories** — group items (Starters, Mains, Drinks…), set their order.
- **Items** — name, description, **price**, photo, and flags for **Dine‑in** and/or **Takeout**.
- **Availability toggle** — mark an item available/unavailable.
- **“86 for service”** — temporarily mark an item out (e.g. you’ve run out tonight). Set an **auto‑reset time** and it comes back automatically. Great for daily specials and stockouts.

Menu prices are what guests pay you **at the venue** — Bucr doesn’t collect the bill.

## 13. Featured Spots (ads) — *Elite*

**Featured Spots** is Bucr’s advertising marketplace — pay (with credits) to put your venue in front of more diners. Access requires the **Elite** tier; you then buy individual placements.

### Packages (set by Bucr; example ladder)
| Type | Package | Duration | Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| Restaurant | **Spotlight** | 3 days | 800 cr (₦8,000) |
| Restaurant | **Prime** ⭐ | 7 days | 1,500 cr (₦15,000) |
| Restaurant | **Headline** | 30 days | 5,000 cr (₦50,000) |
| Experience | **Experience Feature** | 7 days | 1,000 cr (₦10,000) |
| Offer | **Offer Boost** | 7 days | 750 cr (₦7,500) |

Longer durations cost less per day. A featured restaurant appears in the **home carousel** and gets **lifted in search**.

### How to use it
1. Open **Featured Spots** — your credit balance is shown top‑right.
2. Pick a package → **Purchase** (credits are deducted from your wallet).
3. Your **Active Featured Spots** show live **performance metrics**: **Views**, **Clicks**, and **CTR** (click‑through rate) — so you can see exactly what your ad is doing.
4. Toggle **Auto‑renew** on a spot to have it repurchase automatically when it expires (you’re emailed on each renewal, or if it lapses for low credits).

> Impressions are counted fairly (a view only counts when your card is genuinely on‑screen, deduplicated per viewer) so your CTR is real.

## 15. Credits & your vendor wallet

Your **vendor wallet** holds credits. **1 credit = ₦10.**

**You earn credits by:**
- **No‑show compensation** — 30% of a forfeited deposit lands here automatically.
- **Bucr promotions.**

**You spend credits on:**
- **Featured Spots** (ads) and promoted placements.

**You can also top up** your marketing budget by **buying credits with naira** (via Paystack, same 6% spread as guests) on the **Credits** page.

> **Important (and by design):** vendor credits are **marketing credits** — they power your visibility on Bucr. They are **never withdrawable to cash**. This keeps Bucr a simple, licence‑free platform for everyone.

## 18. Reliability & Book‑with‑Confidence

Bucr tracks your **reliability score** (a 90‑day rolling measure of vendor‑side reliability — it counts *your* cancellations/failures, **not** guest no‑shows). Sustain **95%+ for 60 days** and you earn the **Book‑with‑Confidence** badge, shown prominently to diners. It’s a powerful trust signal — protect it by honouring every confirmed booking.

## 19. Money

In plain English, here’s every way money moves:

| Event | What happens |
|---|---|
| Guest books | Guest’s **deposit** (credits) is held by the platform. Nothing leaves your pocket. |
| Guest checks in | Guest gets their deposit back **+3%**. A **per‑cover fee accrues per seated head × party size** (₦1,500/head Basic · ₦750 Pro · ₦0 Elite) — a table of 2 = 2× the fee. |
| Guest no‑shows | Guest forfeits 40%. **You receive 30% of the deposit** as marketing credits. |
| Guest cancels | Refunded per policy (24h+ 100% · 12–24h 50% · <12h 0%). Nothing charged to you. |
| **You (the vendor) cancel** | Guest gets a **100% refund + a 10% compensation bonus, and that 10% is paid from your marketing wallet.** You must hold enough credits (≥10% of the deposit) or the cancellation is blocked. Keep a small credit balance so you can always cancel if you must. |
| The meal bill | Paid **directly to you at the venue** — Bucr never touches it. |
| Monthly | Bucr **invoices you** for accrued per‑cover fees + your subscription. |

**Key principle:** money only ever flows **to Bucr** (fees, subscriptions, ad credits) — Bucr never holds or settles your meal revenue. That’s what keeps the platform simple and licence‑free.

## 20. FAQ

**Do I pay anything to join?** No. Basic is free forever. You only pay per‑cover fees when guests actually show up (and Pro/Elite if you choose to upgrade).

**What if a guest disputes a no‑show?** Mark only genuine no‑shows. Be fair — repeated unfair no‑show marking hurts the guest and your reputation.

**Can I withdraw my credits as cash?** No — vendor credits are marketing credits, by design. Use them for featured placement, or buy more with naira to advertise.

**How do guests pay for the food?** Directly to you at the venue, exactly as they do today. Bucr only handles the reservation deposit (in credits).

**How is the per‑cover fee calculated?** It’s **per head**: base fee × your tier × the party size. A check‑in for 1 guest is 1× the fee; a table of 4 is 4×. Basic = ₦1,500/head, Pro = ₦750/head, Elite = ₦0.

**When do I get the per‑cover fee invoice?** Monthly. Fees accrue automatically each time you check a guest in.

**What happens if I cancel a confirmed booking?** The guest gets a full refund plus a 10% compensation bonus, and **that 10% comes out of your marketing wallet**. If your wallet doesn’t have enough credits, you won’t be able to cancel — so keep a small balance. (Guest‑side cancellations never cost you anything.)

**How do I get more bookings?** Great photos + complete profile → respond to reviews → keep your reliability high (earn the badge) → run Special Offers → buy Featured Spots (Elite).
